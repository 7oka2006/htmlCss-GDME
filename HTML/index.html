What is Ai?
AI (Artificial Intelligence) is the ability of machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. It includes technologies like machine learning, neural networks, and natural language processing. AI is used in voice assistants, self-driving cars, and medical diagnostics.
